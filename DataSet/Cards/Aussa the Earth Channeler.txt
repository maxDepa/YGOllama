Aussa the Earth Channeler
Card Type: Effect Monster

Description: (This card is always treated as a "Charmer" card.)
You can discard this card and 1 EARTH monster; add 1 EARTH monster from your Deck to your hand with 1850 or less ATK, whose original Type is the same as one of the discarded monsters, also you cannot activate non-EARTH monster effects for the rest of this turn. When your EARTH monster is destroyed by battle: You can Special Summon this card from your hand. You can only use each effect of "Aussa the Earth Channeler" once per turn.

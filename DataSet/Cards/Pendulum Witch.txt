Pendulum Witch
Card Type: Pendulum Effect Monster

Description: [ Pendulum Effect ] 
If a face-up Fusion, Synchro, and/or Xyz Monster(s) you control is destroyed by battle or an opponent's card effect while this card is in the Pendulum Zone: You can add 1 Pendulum Monster with the same original Type as 1 of those monsters, from your Deck to your face-up Extra Deck. You can only use this effect of "Pendulum Witch" once per turn.

[ Monster Effect ] 
If this card is Normal or Pendulum Summoned: You can target 1 card in your Pendulum Zone; destroy both that target and this card, and if you do, add 1 Level 4 or lower Pendulum Monster from your Deck to your hand. You can only use this effect of "Pendulum Witch" once per turn. If this card in the Monster Zone is destroyed: You can place this card in your Pendulum Zone.

Dimonno Vaalmonica
Card Type: Pendulum Effect Monster

Description: [ Pendulum Effect ] 
Each time you take effect damage while you control a Fairy Monster Card in your other Pendulum Zone, place 1 Resonance Counter on this card. Monsters your opponent controls lose 100 ATK for each Resonance Counter on your field.

[ Monster Effect ] 
If this card is in your hand: You can discard 1 other card; take 1 "Angello Vaalmonica" from your Deck, and place this card and that card in your Pendulum Zones. During your Main Phase, if this card was Normal or Special Summoned this turn: You can banish 1 "Vaalmonica" Normal Spell/Trap from your GY; apply whichever effect on that card includes taking damage. You can only use each effect of "Dimonno Vaalmonica" once per turn.
